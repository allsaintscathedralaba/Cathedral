Our Early Beginnings

All Saints' Cathedral, Abayi-Umuocham had its humble beginnings in the early 20th century as a small fellowship of dedicated Anglican believers gathering in family compounds. Over the decades, through tireless evangelism, community commitment, and the blessings of God, the fellowship expanded into a full parish.

With the creation of the Diocese of Aba Ngwa North (Anglican Communion), the parish was chosen to serve as the seat of the Bishop, elevate to a Cathedral Status, representing structural, theological, and evangelistic leadership across the diocese.

Mission Statement

To build a vibrant, Bible-based, and holy congregation of believers through intensive evangelism, solid Christian education, and sincere welfare.

Vision Statement

To remain a leading Cathedral in Nigeria, raising generations of spiritually matured Christians who represent excellence in their families and societies.
