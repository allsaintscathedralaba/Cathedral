Privacy Policy

Learn how we collect, store, secure, and manage your personal data across our digital platforms.

1. Introduction

All Saints' Cathedral Abayi-Umuocham ("the Cathedral", "we", "us", or "our"), under the Diocese of Aba Ngwa North (Anglican Communion), is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or interact with us through contact forms, newsletter subscriptions, or online media.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide voluntarily (such as full name, email address, phone number, and message content when submitting contact or membership forms). We also collect general anonymous traffic data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) through basic analytical scripts to improve the website's performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to respond to your queries, process membership requests, send weekly devotions or newsletters (if subscribed), optimize user interface design, and track overall page visitors. We will never sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties.

4. Security of Data

All data transactions between your browser and our servers are encrypted using standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL/HTTPS) configurations. We implement strict internal access controls to ensure that only authorized administrative staff can view submitted form logs.

5. Contact & Support

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, wish to update your records, or request deletion of your submitted forms, please email our IT Support desk at: info@allsaintscathedralaba.org.
